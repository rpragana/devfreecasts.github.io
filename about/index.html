About

This site is a open-source initiative whose the goal is sharing only free dev screencasts for everybody. If you love Node.js, Go, JavaScript, Ruby or any other programming language or framework, and you have some screencasts sharing your basic or advanced knowledge, please send it to us! Will be a honor to fill up this site with a huge collection of free dev videos.

Terms

We do not promote piracy here, all videos are free to watch. We do not store user data and the video's links are redirected to the sites of the authors. If any content of this site violates the copyright, please contact me, to request the content's deletion.
